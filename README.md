## Problem Statement
Specially abled individuals face barriers such as inaccessible polling booths,
lack of assistive technologies, and dependence on third parties, compromising
privacy and independence in voting.

## Our Solution
VoteAble is an accessible and secure digital voting platform designed to enable
specially abled citizens to vote independently using assistive technologies
like voice commands, keyboard-only navigation, and screen-reader support.
